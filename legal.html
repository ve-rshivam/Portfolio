// Terms & Conditions

To ensure a smooth workflow and avoid misunderstandings, the following terms apply to all freelance projects:

  • Clear Scope of Work: All features and requirements must be documented before starting. If new features are requested after the project begins (Scope Creep), they will be estimated and billed separately.
  • Revisions & Iterations: You are entitled to up to 3 rounds of revisions for design/development tweaks. Complete redesigns or changes outside the original scope will incur additional charges.
  • Intellectual Property (IP) & Keys Transfer: I retain full ownership of all code, designs, API keys, and assets created until the final payment is cleared. Once the final payment is successfully received, all agreed-upon IP rights, ownership, and credentials will be fully transferred to you.
  • Communication & Ghosting: Timely feedback is crucial. If a client fails to communicate for more than 14 days without prior notice, the project will be put on hold, and a restart fee may apply to resume work.
